Project Ideas

Our project will be a web game name Bouncing Ball based on the Ping Pong Game [1] which is Minh’s idea. The game will have a ball and a bar, each time the ball bounces, the player gets one score, after a certain amount of time or the ball hits the bottom, the total score will be awarded. There will be some development within the project depending on the skill sets of each team member, adding a leaderboard for friends to compete for fun, random obstacles for the ball to hit, sound animation, maybe a Christmas theme and much more. This project's aim is to help people relax after a tough day by looking at the bouncing ball and getting a score.

Feedback
"The project idea was sparked and developed by Minh himself. The first game concept is quite basic and easy to interact with yet somehow, it is still very interesting and fun to spend your leisure time on for a few rounds. Hence, it will be the kind of game that is both relaxing and entertaining to play! However, the background theme is a bit hard to separate itself from the interactive objects. Furthermore, the game had started on its own before I could read any instructions, so it was a bit confusing at first. I recommend we have a start button, a pause button, a continue button and some warning signs when the levels get higher. Additionally, we can add more features to the game, such as boost-up potion for the bat; or to make it more challenging, we could add obstacles like random objects popping out, etc. Lastly, I suggest we have a little touch on both the designs of the layout and the interactive objects. Since the visuals, which will be what attract and keep the users around, are also important. One of the re-touches should be the background itself, we can change to something duller on the color, yet still keep the theme of Christmas; we should also give the players a variety choice of costumes for the balls and bats so that they can choose as they please. All in all, I personally believe that the project’s first completion is brilliant and with just a little more adjustment as I mentioned, opinions and contributions from the rest of the teammates, the game will stand out and might as well become more completed and unique on its own." - Doanh

"Minh's suggestion came as the inspiration for our project's concept. Although the first game concept is basic and easy to play, I believe it has potential for further development. I simply wanted to provide some suggestions for our current project. First and foremost, I believe that we should customize our game by enabling players to choose their own themes using more bright graphics and colors. Next, we should make it multiplatform accessible, so that users can easily play it on a variety of various devices and platforms. Finally, organizing a competition with an attractive reward might stimulate people's interest in our game and make them more interested in it." - Khuong

"Our project was based on Minh’s idea. Personally, I think Minh’s project is a clever project which can mitigate the competitiveness between the players. Though I think the project should have some different game modes such as most points scored during a specific time or a 2 versus 2 mode to have more attractiveness. In addition, we should add some bar skins to brings positive experience for the players. Furthermore, a weekly contest for a group of friends with a reward is worth to bring in consider. From my perspective, I think my ideas will help the project to improve its friendliness to enhance the user’s experience to the fullest." - Kiet
